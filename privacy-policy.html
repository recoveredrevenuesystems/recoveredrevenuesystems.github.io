Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Recovered Revenue Systems ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website https://recoverprofitsnow.com and provides AI-powered SMS automation services to home service businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a demo request form on our website (name, email address, business name, industry)
  • Call our business phone number
  • Communicate with us via SMS, email, or phone

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Contact you to schedule or conduct a product demo
  • Send you SMS messages related to your inquiry or service request
  • Provide and improve our services
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Send service-related communications

3. SMS Communications

By submitting our demo request form or calling our business number, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Recovered Revenue Systems. These messages may include:

  • Responses to missed calls
  • Follow-up messages related to your inquiry
  • Appointment reminders and service notifications

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message. Reply HELP for assistance. For support, contact us at vanroekelsteve@gmail.com.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our business (such as SMS delivery platforms)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

8. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies, though some parts of our site may not function properly as a result.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us
